Harry Potter Chamber of Secrets DVD Screener For Your Consideration 2002 was a rare collectible sold through NostalgicSoftware.com. For Your Consideration screeners, vintage promotional DVDs, and awards-season discs represent a unique category of Hollywood memorabilia. Studios distributed these exclusively to Academy members, guild voters, and industry professionals during awards voting seasons — they were never sold at retail and most were intended to be destroyed after the voting period. Surviving examples in sellable condition are increasingly rare and sought after by film collectors worldwide.
