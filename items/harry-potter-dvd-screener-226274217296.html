// About This Item

The Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets For Your Consideration DVD Screener was a rare promotional disc distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures during the 2002–2003 awards season. These screeners were sent exclusively to Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences members, Golden Globe voters, and other qualifying industry guilds to encourage consideration for award nominations — hence the "For Your Consideration" designation printed on the disc and packaging.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets was released theatrically in November 2002, directed by Chris Columbus and starring Daniel Radcliffe, Emma Watson, and Rupert Grint in their return to the beloved J.K. Rowling wizarding world. The film was a massive commercial and critical success, grossing over $878 million worldwide and receiving multiple technical award nominations including a BAFTA nomination for Best Special Visual Effects.

For Your Consideration DVD screeners from this era are highly collectible. Studios produced limited quantities for awards distribution only — they were never sold at retail. Most were marked with unique serial numbers or watermarks to track distribution. Finding one in resellable condition is increasingly rare, as many were destroyed after the voting season ended per studio policy. This disc represents a piece of Hollywood history from a landmark franchise.
